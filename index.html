Welcome to Wizards Wrestling

The Crystal Lake Wizards Wrestling Club is a youth and amateur wrestling program based in Crystal Lake, Illinois. We are dedicated to developing athletes of all skill levels — from beginners to competitive wrestlers — through hard work, discipline, and teamwork.

Whether you're stepping onto the mat for the first time or preparing for a tournament, the Wizards are your home. Our experienced coaches focus on technique, character, and competitive excellence.

New and returning members are always welcome. Come join the family — practice times and registration information are available on the Contact Us page.
