⚙ Site Admin: How to Activate Stripe Payment Links

  1. Create a free Stripe account at stripe.com. Apply for nonprofit rates under Settings → Business details — as a 501(c)(3) you qualify for 2.2% + 30¢ instead of 2.9% + 30¢.
  2. In your Stripe dashboard, go to Payment Links → + Create link. Set the price to $150 and name it White Sponsorship. Copy the link URL (looks like https://buy.stripe.com/abc123).
  3. Repeat step 2 for each tier: Black $250, Yellow $500, Platinum $1,000, Title $1,500, VIP Corporate $2,500.
  4. Open sponsors.html in a text editor. Find each <a href="#stripe-setup-required"> button and replace #stripe-setup-required with the matching Stripe Payment Link URL. Also remove the onclick="alert(...)" attribute from each button.
  5. Once all 6 links are replaced, you can optionally delete this entire <div class="setup-box"> section from the page — it's only needed during setup.
  6. Save the file, commit, and push. Sponsors will be taken directly to Stripe’s hosted checkout. Stripe sends them a receipt automatically, and deposits funds to your connected bank account.
