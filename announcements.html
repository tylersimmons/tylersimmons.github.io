Welcome Back, Families!

August 2025
Announcement

We are so thrilled to kick off the new school year! It is going to be a wonderful year full of learning, laughter, and lots of fun. A few reminders as we get started:

Fall Theme: Harvest & Friends

October 2025
Classroom News

This fall our classroom theme is Harvest & Friends! We'll be exploring pumpkins, apples, colorful leaves, and what it means to be a kind friend. Expect lots of sensory bins, art projects, and read-alouds centered around the season.

We'd love donations of small gourds or mini pumpkins for our sensory table — drop them in the donation basket outside our classroom door. Thank you!

From Mrs. Simmons

Parent-Teacher Conferences

November 2025
Event

Parent-teacher conferences will be held in November. Sign-up sheets will go home in October. These are a wonderful opportunity to celebrate your child's growth and share any questions you have about their development.

Conferences are approximately 15 minutes each. If the available times don't work for you, please reach out and we'll find a time that does!

From Mrs. Simmons

Snack Policy Reminder

Ongoing
Reminder

Just a friendly reminder about our snack guidelines: please send a nut-free snack in a labeled bag each day. Great options include:

  • Fresh fruit or veggies
  • String cheese or yogurt pouches
  • Crackers or pretzels
  • Applesauce pouches

Please avoid candy, chips, or heavily sweetened items. Thank you!

From Mrs. Simmons
