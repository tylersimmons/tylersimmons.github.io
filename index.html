About Our Classroom

Where every day is an adventure in learning.

Hello, families! I'm so excited to be your child's teacher this year. In our classroom, we believe that the best learning happens through play, exploration, and connection. Every activity we do is designed to help your child build confidence, curiosity, and friendships.

"Every child is a unique gift, and it's my joy to help them discover the world around them — one crayon, one story, and one belly laugh at a time."

At St. Peter Lutheran, we're rooted in a community of faith and care. I look forward to partnering with you throughout this school year. Please don't hesitate to reach out anytime!
